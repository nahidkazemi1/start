Brown Horse

There are many things to consider when naming your brown horse. Whether you pay homage to his color, his personality or just get really creative, make sure you love what you pick.


Names Based on Shades of Brown

It makes sense to want to name your horse based on its color. There are many shades of brown and many names to give each shade. Decide which one suits your horse best based on his shade of brown. Clay or Clayton Carob - A tree produces beans that look like brown snap peas Brunneous - Shade of dark brown Fuscous - A literary term for brownish-gray Bister - Brownish pigment with yellow undertones made from soot of burned wood
